About Me

Hello! I am a first-year graduate student at Virginia Tech, pursuing a Ph.D. in Theoretical Chemistry. I am especially interested in codes and methods development. I am working in the Valeev Group.

I've developed an interest in coding, as a professional skill and as a hobby. I hope to make a career combining my interests in programming and the hard sciences.

I am currently funded by a Doctoral Scholarship from ICTAS (Institute for Critical Technology and Applied Science) at VT.
