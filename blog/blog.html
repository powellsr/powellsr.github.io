Blog Page

Welcome to my blog main page. Don't know what I'll write here. Most of the blogs I've read and appreciated contain important or useful technical information, which I don't know any of to share.

I could also use it to share my opinions and participate in the broader social discourse using the inspiring and powerful technology that is the internet, I think I'm 15-20 years too late for that. I think the forum for that nowadays is probably Twitter, but I find that rather vapid and certainly not inspiring. Besides, this venture could be rather dangerous, as I've been treating this as more of a professional site. Well, at any rate, this discussion is rather moot until I at least get to the point of getting this up on the main branch.

I may also use it to sandbox different designs and subdomain-stuff.
